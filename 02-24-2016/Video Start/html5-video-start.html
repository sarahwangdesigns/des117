HTML5 Video Demo

Video playback is one of the most important new features in HTML5. It allows us to play video from the built-in player rather than depend on a third party player like Quicktime, Flash or Windows Movie Player.

The control attribute adds video controls, like play, pause, and volume. You can also include autoplay and loop or a combination of commands. It is a good idea to always include width and height attributes. If height and width are set, the space required for the video is reserved when the page is loaded. However, without these attributes, the browser does not know the size of the video, and cannot reserve the appropriate space to it. The effect will be that the page layout will change during loading (while the video loads).

You should also insert text content between the video and /video tags for browsers that do not support the video element.

The video element allows multiple source elements. source elements can link to different video files. The browser will use the first recognized format. Keep in mind that browsers are not fully compliant with one video file type so best to use at least
