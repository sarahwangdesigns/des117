SmileyToast

Start your morning off with a toast for happiness with SmileyToast!




SmileyToast is a transparent toaster that will toast your toast perfectly to your liking.

The transparency of SmileyToast also allows you to better monitor how toast your toast gets, better ensuring a perfect toast in your definitions and to your preference. All functions of the toaster are applicable with a touch of the transparent screen.

Not only does SmileyToast have a built in clock, but it has six standard settings for how toasty you would like your toast. If those six standard settings don't satisfy you, you can create your own custom settings too, from level of toastiness you want your toast to get, to the temperature you want your toast to toast at and for how long the toast should toast for. In addition, SmileyToast also acts as a cooking timer. SmileyToast will alert you when your toast or timer is done with a sound or song of your choice, or no sound at all.

SmileyToast also has an option of imprinting your toast with a happy face, a optional feature added to promote mental awareness. Therefore, fore every SmileyToast purchased,SmileyToast will donate to the Trevor Project, a national organization dedicated to providing crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender teens and young adults.

SmileyToast indefinitely will bring you smiles in the morning by making you your perfect toast every time and remind you that having a healthy state of mind everyday is important!
